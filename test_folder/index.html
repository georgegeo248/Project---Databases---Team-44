Welcome to the H.F.R.I. database

The Hellenic Foundation for Research and Innovation's goals are the promotion of research and innovation in Greece
through funding research activities and technological applications assessed solely on the basis of scientific excellence.
The Foundation was created out of the vital need to support young scientists and as a necessary tool in reversing the flow of outstanding scientists out of Greece.
